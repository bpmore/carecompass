Frequently Asked Questions

What’s included in the free consultation?

Our 30-minute consultation assesses your loved one’s needs, outlines a basic care plan, and provides 2–3 vetted care options, like in-home care or facilities such as Vitality Living Pleasant Hills.

How do you differ from national agencies?

Unlike national services, we’re Little Rock-based, offering unbiased recommendations with no facility affiliations. Our partnerships with UAMS and Baptist Health ensure local expertise.

Can you help with Medicaid or VA benefits?

Yes! We specialize in Arkansas’s Living Choices Waiver and VA Aid and Attendance, streamlining applications to save you thousands, as we did for John in Maumelle.

What if my needs change over time?

Our Ongoing Support plan ($200/month) provides monthly check-ins and care plan adjustments to adapt to evolving needs, ensuring long-term peace of mind.
